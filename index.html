Crystal Collector

How to Play


  1. You/'ll be given a random number.
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Click a crystal to add points to your score
  4. You win if your score matches the number
  5. You lose if your score goes over the number
  6. The value of the crystals are hidden
  7. The value of the crystals are different every game
